Lux Corrigan is a composer who focuses on creating scores for concert and film. Lux combines their skills to create music that can support the image of screen or paint a picture in the mind. While primarily a composer, Lux provides services in orchestration, engraving, and sound design as well.

Based in Chicago, Lux is ready to collaborate with you, providing the signature sound of your film, orchestrating your MIDI score, or creating an original commission for your ensemble.
