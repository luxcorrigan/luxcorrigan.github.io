Lux Corrigan is a Chicago-based composer and orchestrator creating original music for concert settings and visual media. With a background in classical composition and narrative sound design, Lux mixes traditional work with modern techniques to provide an sound for your project.

Background

Lux Corrigan became involved with music when they began playing violin in elementary school. In middle and high school they became involved in both band as a euphonium player and choir as a vocalist. Lux attended Plainfield North High School where they developed a passion for marching band, there they also began their study in music theory. Lux was involved with organizations such as Tri-M National Music Honors Society and Chromium Winds, a WGI indoor winds group.

Lux attributes their earliest compositional influences to many genres, specifically, they were a young fan of Broadway shows and movie musicals. They also loved the music and scores of many animated movies, as well as fantasy and sci-fi films. Their time in scholastic bands and choirs greatly influences the style of music they compose.

Approach & Philosophy

Lux believes that music and visual story-telling are integral to one-another. When writing concert work, their mission is tell a story using the sheet music as their script and the performers as their actors.

This philosophy extends to the collaborative process of visual media. Lux believes that the best creative work emerges from clear vision of the story that is being told and that music and sound are key tools to enhace and communicate the story.

Current Practice

Today, Lux is based in Chicago, finishing their BA at Columbia College Chicago while working on personal projects, scholastic level compositions and completing commissions.

Lux believes in the importance of education and remains committed to continuous learning, regularly exploring new compositional techniques and tools. Lux has plans to explore graduate school and other formal training to continue improving and expanding their skills to ensure the highest quality of work.
