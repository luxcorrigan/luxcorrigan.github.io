A Voice

Lux Corrigan is a Chicago-based composer and orchestrator creating distinctive sonic landscapes for film, theater, and concert works. With a background in classical composition and digital sound design, Lux brings a unique perspective to contemporary music.

Background

Lux Corrigan discovered music early, spending childhood years exploring both acoustic instruments and early digital audio workstations. This dual foundation—rooted in classical training yet shaped by contemporary production techniques—became the cornerstone of a compositional philosophy that honors tradition while embracing innovation.

Growing up in a creative household, Lux was exposed to the full spectrum of musical storytelling: from orchestral film scores to experimental electronic composition. This breadth of influence instilled a deep appreciation for how music can transcend genre and purpose, functioning equally as a standalone art form or as a powerful accompaniment to visual narratives.

Training & Education

Formal Study

  • BA in Music Composition for Media - Columbia College Chicago
  • Associate of Arts in Music - Joliet Junior College
  • Certificate of Completion in Music Technology - Joliet Junior College
  • Additional Study in Music Theory, Composition, and Orchestration - Coe College

Mentorship & Professional Development

Beyond formal education, Lux has worked alongside accomplished composers and orchestrators, learning the craft through direct collaboration. These mentorships provided invaluable insights into professional practice, from meeting deadline pressures to navigating the creative demands of collaborative projects with directors, producers, and ensemble musicians.

Career & Achievements

Lux's professional practice spans multiple domains. As a composer, Lux has created original scores for independent films, theatrical productions, and concert works performed by regional orchestras and contemporary ensembles. As an orchestrator, Lux has realized MIDI sketches into full orchestral arrangements, working with both traditional notation software and innovative digital tools.

Notable Work

Recent projects include orchestral commissions for chamber and full orchestra, film scores for documentaries and narrative features, and sound design contributions to interactive media projects. Each project reflects Lux's commitment to creating music that serves its purpose—whether that's elevating an emotional moment on screen or standing alone as a concert work.

Recognition

Lux's work has been recognized for its orchestration clarity, harmonic sophistication, and ability to convey complex emotional landscapes. Collaborators consistently appreciate Lux's responsiveness to creative feedback and professional approach to deadline-driven projects.

Approach & Philosophy

"Music is not decoration. It's language—a way of speaking when words aren't enough."

Lux approaches every project with a deep respect for the source material and a commitment to serving the broader creative vision. Whether scoring a film, orchestrating a composer's work, or creating an original concert piece, the guiding principle is always the same: clarity of purpose, depth of expression, and technical excellence.

This philosophy extends to the collaborative process. Lux believes that the best creative work emerges from clear communication, mutual respect, and a shared commitment to the project's success. As both a solo artist and a collaborator, Lux brings professionalism, creativity, and a genuine passion for the power of music to move audiences.

Current Practice

Today, Lux is based in Chicago, finishing their BA at Columbia College Chicago while working on personal projects, scholastic level compositions and completing commissions.

Lux remains committed to continuous learning, regularly exploring new compositional techniques, production workflows, and tools that expand creative possibilities. Whether commissioned for a specific project or creating music for its own sake, Lux's work is always driven by a deep respect for the craft and a desire to create music that resonates—music that listeners remember, that enhances stories, and that stands the test of time.
